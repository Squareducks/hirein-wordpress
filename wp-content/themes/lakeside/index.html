SCAFFOLD TOWER HIRE

Now you don’t need to have a big corporate account to get great rates on hire of Aluminium Scaffold Tower Hire, Site Box Hire, Razor Deck, Podium Steps,  and Access Equipment in London, Essex, Manchester, Liverpool, Birmingham, Leeds and nationwide.

Scaffold Towers and Scaffold Tower Hire

Aluminium Scaffold Towers are ideal for providing a stable and secure platform for all trades and also domestic work. Ideal for site work, the highly versatile BOSS tower has a proven track record in the construction industry. Our aluminum scaffold towers are available double and single width, in stair tower and also in GRP (fibreglass) for electrical contractors.

Podium Steps

Access platform details – Podium Steps Podium steps have become a must on most major construction sites. But due to their construction just stocking one type of podium isn’t going to cover all types of work a contractor will do. With this in mind Lakeside-hire have invested in a good podium steps range to make sure customers are always working at the correct platform height. Not only does this speed up work rate and efficiency but also reflects well on the contractors health and safety image. If you need a larger podium steps we supply podiums up to 2.45m platform height. Have a look at our full range.

Site Box

Hire Site Boxes – The Site Box provides maximum storage space on site and is designed to be virtually impenetrable. Round the clock security is no guarantee of protecting valuable tools. The hire of site boxes helps combat the rising tide of theft from construction sites and other workplaces.

Razor Deck

There are no loose components with the Razor Deck. The fail-safe, 1-piece design calls for minimal training. The unit unfolds from a compact easily manoeuvred base and simply snap locks into place at the desired height. Adjustable integral outriggers provide additional stability and the ample deck provides space and support for the operative and tools. Very good alternative to low level scaffold tower. Visit our razor deck page for more information.
